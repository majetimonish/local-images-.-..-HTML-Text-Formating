Sajja Roti

Description

Sajja Roti is a traditional millet-based flatbread that has been a part of Guntur’s culinary heritage for generations. Made from pearl millet (sajja) flour, this roti has a slightly nutty flavor and is often enjoyed with curries and chutneys. Millet flour is known for its health benefits, offering a nutritious alternative to wheat.

To prepare Sajja Roti, millet flour is kneaded into a soft dough with water and a pinch of salt. The dough is rolled into thin discs and cooked on a griddle until golden brown. Sajja Roti is a popular choice for those seeking a wholesome meal that aligns with Guntur’s dedication to healthy, plant-based dishes.

Recipe

Ingredients

• 1 cup millet (sajja) flour • Salt to taste • Water as needed

Instructions

• Mix flour with salt and water, knead into a dough. • Roll into flatbreads, cook on a tawa until golden brown. • Serve with your favorite curry.
