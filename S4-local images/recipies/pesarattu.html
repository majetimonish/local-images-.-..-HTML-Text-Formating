Pesarattu

Description

Pesarattu is a protein-packed dosa made from green gram (moong dal), a unique twist that sets it apart from traditional dosas. This Andhra classic is popular for its health benefits and distinct flavor, offering a crisp, savory pancake that’s ideal for breakfast or a light meal. It’s typically topped with finely chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger, adding an extra layer of flavor and texture.

The process starts by soaking green gram and rice, which are then ground into a smooth batter with a hint of ginger for added flavor. The batter is spread on a hot griddle, and as it cooks, onions and green chilies are sprinkled on top. The result is a crispy, flavorful dosa that pairs wonderfully with ginger chutney or tamarind chutney. Pesarattu showcases the versatility of green gram and is loved by both health-conscious individuals and food enthusiasts alike.

Recipe

Ingredients

• 1 cup green gram • 1/2 cup rice • 1-inch ginger • Salt to taste • Chopped onions and green chilies for topping

Instructions

• Soak green gram and rice for 4 hours, then grind with ginger to a smooth batter. • Spread batter on a hot pan, top with onions and chilies. • Cook until crispy, then serve with ginger chutney.
